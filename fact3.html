Greenland was founded in 982 by the Norwegian, you know him, Erik the Red.

The meaning of the flag of Greenland is as follows.
The red color, called Aappalaartoq, is used in bothe the Greenland and Danish flag.
The red and white of the flag symbolize the hundreds of years of being a part of the Kingdom of Denmark.
The Circle in the middle represents the sun setting on the horizon,
And the light and warmth that return at midsummer.

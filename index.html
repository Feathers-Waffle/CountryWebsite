Greenland was founded in 982 by the Norwegian Erik the Red, after he was banished from Iceland for mansloughter.
The current population of Greenland is 56,653 people, as of 2021.
Greenlands official language, is of course Greenlandic. 70% of the people speak only Greenlandic,
But the political and administrative elites predominantly speak Danish.
